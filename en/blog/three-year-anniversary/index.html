Three-year anniversary at Consensys

8 March 2025

I joined Consensys three years ago this week. And what an amazing journey it has been! Never a dull day in the crypto space, always something new to discover.

From my very first day, when my manager asked me to integrate Bitcoin into a DeFi project (I only knew Ethereum at the time) to joining the Linea team, I’ve worked on countless products and with so many teams. But one thing has always remained the same: the passion, the drive, and the incredibly smart people everywhere. I’m humbled by the people around me, hard workers, performance-driven but also extremely human and approachable. Colleagues turned into friends, and I’m forever grateful for the memories we create every day.

And the trips! I’ve never travelled as much as with Consensys. Ethereum conferences to showcase our products all over the world, from Denver to Bangkok. Off-sites to meet face-to-face and build stronger bonds, from Lisbon to Palermo. I’ve also recently learned to connect more with those who build in this space, especially on social media.

I got the opportunity to shape and build Verax, the best project I’ve ever worked on, by far. Bringing verifiable credentials to the EVM world is a challenge but also a game-changer for how we prove things onchain.

Over the past three years, I’ve seen the ecosystem mature and lay the foundations for the future. Scaling Ethereum is still in progress, but Linea and other rollups are getting there. Wallets evolved so much, and MetaMask is still a leader in its industry. The infrastructure is finally catching up with the vision, and it’s exciting to be part of it.

I feel like I learn something new every day. I get to meet the community building on Linea. I wake up excited to get to work every single day. How amazing is that? How lucky am I!
