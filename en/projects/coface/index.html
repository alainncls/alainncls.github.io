Coface

January 2016 - December 2016

Client's website

Project's website

The client

Coface is a major player in credit insurance. In order to remain competitive and gain new market share, the company needs to regularly offer new software products and solutions to its customers.

The project

Optimization and evolution of a web application (Cofanet Policy Master & Cofanet Policy Master Administration) for paying customers.

To achieve this, a technical refactoring of the project architecture and a redesign of the major features of the application were needed.

In addition to the technical aspects, I had the opportunity to accompany the transition to Agile mode (Scrum) throughout the project, as well as to weigh in on the design decisions to be implemented.

Technical stack

Client-side

JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Ajax, Bootstrap

Back-end

Spring Service / Spring MVC based on Java 6 before migration to Java 8

Database

OracleDB
