eFrogs Attestation

April 2024 - Present

eFrogs Attestation: Prove your Membership on Linea

Do you own eFrogs on Linea and want to prove your membership in this amazing community? With eFrogs Attestation, you can now do so using Verax.

Project Context

In April 2024, this project was created during a hackathon hosted by Linea and eFrogs. Developed in collaboration with Orion, it won second place in the competition. Initially built as a prototype, the project has evolved into a fully functional app deployed on the mainnet.

Features

Using efrogs.alainnicolas.fr, eFrogs NFT holders can:

  • Generate a public attestation proving ownership of their eFrogs NFTs.

  • Register this attestation onchain via Verax.

  • Enhance their credibility within the eFrogs community.

About Verax

Verax is an onchain attestation registry that enables dApps to store public data, called "attestations," immutably and accessibly. This innovative solution fosters composability and interoperability in the Ethereum ecosystem.

As the tech lead for this project, detailed here, I’m committed to showcasing integration examples of this solution within the web3 ecosystem.

The eFrogs Collection

eFrogs (Ethereum Frogs) is an iconic PFP collection on Linea, featuring 2015 unique NFTs. Beyond its active community, it has inspired several derivative projects, such as:

  • The $CROAK token

  • The eFroglets collection

  • The Pond.fun launchpad

 I’m not affiliated with eFrogs or part of the team behind this ecosystem. However, I’m part of the community and appreciate their work. Also, this is obviously not financial advice.

Why This Project?

This project primarily aims to explore Verax’s potential and contribute to the eFrogs ecosystem. While it doesn’t serve a specific purpose, it paves the way for innovative use cases, such as soul-bound tokens that allow transferring reputation in case of wallet compromise.

How to Attest Your NFTs

  1. Visit efrogs.alainnicolas.fr.

  2. Connect your wallet holding eFrogs NFTs.

  3. Click "Issue Attestation" to generate and record your attestation via Verax.

In just a few steps, prove your membership in the eFrogs community immutably and securely.
